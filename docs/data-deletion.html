Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: 2025-10-01

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. We will delete linked content, schedules, analytics records, and third‑party tokens associated with your account, subject to legal and security constraints.

How to request deletion

  1. Open a request at our GitHub Issues with the title: Data Deletion Request.
  2. Include the email address linked to your AutoPromote account.
  3. We may verify your identity via a signed‑in session or email verification.

What we delete

  • Firestore user document and linked subcollections (notifications, badges).
  • Content documents, promotion schedules, and executions.
  • Storage media you uploaded (in Firebase Storage under your UID).
  • Third‑party tokens (e.g., TikTok) stored server‑side for your UID.

Timeline

We aim to complete deletion within 30 days of verification, subject to system backups and legal holds.

Contact

If you cannot access GitHub, contact the email listed in the app to initiate a deletion request.
