Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-01

AutoPromote ("we", "us") helps creators schedule and promote content across platforms. This policy describes what we collect, why, and how you can exercise your rights.

What we collect

  • Account data: email, display name, and authentication identifiers (via Firebase Auth).
  • Content metadata: titles, descriptions, target platforms, and media URLs (stored in Firebase Storage and Firestore).
  • Scheduling and analytics data: promotion schedules, execution logs, basic engagement metrics.
  • Optional third‑party tokens: if you connect external platforms (e.g., TikTok), we store access tokens securely in server‑side storage only.

How we use data

  • Provide and improve the service (scheduling, landing pages, smart links, analytics).
  • Security, fraud prevention, and troubleshooting.
  • Communications related to your account and service changes.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers strictly to operate the service (e.g., Firebase, Render hosting). We may disclose information if required by law.

Data retention

We retain data while your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter for troubleshooting, backups, and legal compliance. You can request deletion at any time (see below).

Your rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your data.
  • Export a copy of your content and profile data.
  • Withdraw third‑party app connections (revoking tokens).

Children

AutoPromote is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Security

We use Firebase Authentication and Firestore security rules, apply role‑based access checks on our API, and store third‑party tokens only on the server side (never in the client).

Contact

To exercise rights or ask questions, please open a GitHub issue in our repository: https://github.com/Tibule12/AutoPromote/issues or contact the email listed in the app.

Data deletion

See our Data Deletion page for how to request deletion of your account and associated data.
